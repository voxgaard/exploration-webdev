Delusion

Circa 2006

No contract was made, even verbally;

This debt materialized over time —

Forged organically, tempered subconsciously, contoured unfortunately.

Even were you geographically near,

I cannot intervene - the past has cemented my failure.

I attempt to show others the compassion I owed dearly to you,

Trying to restore a shred of faith in myself.

For all I know you're alive and well...

I'm terrified that you're not.

What plagues me now has assured me,

I didn't see your rage.
